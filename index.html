Our Mission

The Powers Project Foundation fundraises and works to build schools and provide quality education to children affected by poverty in East Africa. Education is the key driver in good development and the success of every child's individual future

Arusha Project

The Arusha Project

Arusha is a city located in the Northern region of the stunning East African country, Tanzania. Arusha is a smaller sized city, with a population of approximately 500,000 people, and it is nestled amongst thick greenery and mountains, where it sits at the base of Mt Meru. Arusha is considered the ‘gateway’ city to some of Africas most sought after Safari destinations, including the Serengetti, Tarangire National Park and the Ngorongoro Crater, as well as the world's highest free standing peak, Mt Kilimanjaro.
Arusha is a colourful city with quite a fun and bustling culture, where a sense of community, and love of music and dance
